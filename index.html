About

Welcome to the world of blacksmithing! Here you will find everything you need to know about becoming a blacksmith, from the basic skills to advanced techniques.

Discover the art of shaping metal, creating beautiful and functional objects, and learn about the rich history and traditions of blacksmithing.

Skills

  • Forging techniques
  • Heat treatment
  • Tool making
  • Joinery and welding
  • Design and aesthetics

Training

Whether you're a beginner or have some experience, we offer comprehensive training programs to suit your needs. Our courses cover the following topics:

  • Introduction to blacksmithing
  • Basic forging techniques
  • Advanced projects
  • Specialized workshops

Join us and learn from experienced blacksmiths who are passionate about passing on their knowledge.
